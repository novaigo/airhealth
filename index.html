Here's how to use AirHealth:

Upon your initial visit to our website from a desktop device, your browser may ask to use your location. Please select 'Allow' to enable personalized air quality information based on your current whereabouts. After allowing access to your current location, please allow a few moments for the data to be fetched.

This ensures personalized and accurate data, enhancing your understanding of the local air quality conditions.

When accessing from a mobile device, please enter a specific location to retrieve comprehensive air quality details. For best results, provide precise locations with exact addresses.

Bad Address Example:
Vienna, Austria

Good Address Example:
Stephansplatz 1, 1010 Vienna, Austria
