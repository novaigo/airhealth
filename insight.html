Insight

AirHealth is dedicated to providing current and accessible air pollution data, empowering informed decisions for well-being. As air pollution significantly impacts health and the environment, AirHealth's mission is to raise awareness and enable a better understanding of air quality data for informed health choices.

Utilizing the OpenWeather Air Pollution API and OpenCage Geocoding API, AirHealth offers comprehensive access to current air pollution data worldwide. This dataset encompasses pollutants such as Carbon Monoxide (CO), Nitrogen Monoxide (NO), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2), Ozone (O3), Sulphur Dioxide (SO2), Ammonia (NH3), as well as particulates PM2.5 and PM10.

The user-friendly interface enables easy access to vital information, allowing users to monitor local and global air quality indices and pollutant concentrations.

Users are encouraged to explore air quality indices and pollutant concentrations in multiple locations worldwide through AirHealth. This exploration will provide a comprehensive understanding of air quality conditions in various regions, assisting in making informed decisions for health and well-being.

Here's how to use AirHealth:

Upon your initial visit to our website from a desktop device, your browser may ask to use your location. Please select 'Allow' to enable personalized air quality information based on your current whereabouts. After allowing access to your current location, please allow a few moments for the data to be fetched.

This ensures personalized and accurate data, enhancing your understanding of the local air quality conditions.

When accessing from a mobile device, please enter a specific location to retrieve comprehensive air quality details. For best results, provide precise locations with exact addresses.
